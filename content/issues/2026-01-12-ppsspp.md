We have released a new container for [PPSSPP](https://github.com/linuxserver/docker-ppsspp/)!

[PPSSPP](https://www.ppsspp.org/) is a free and open-source PSP emulator for Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android, Nintendo Wii U, Nintendo Switch, BlackBerry 10, MeeGo, Pandora, Xbox Series and Symbian with a focus on speed and portability.
