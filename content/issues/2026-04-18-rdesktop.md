---
title: 'Rdesktop End of Life Has Arrived'
date: '2026-04-18 21:00:00Z'
informational: true
affected:
  - 'Notifications'
  - 'rdesktop'
section: 'issue'
---

Docker Rdesktop has now been shutdown, If you require RDP access to an isolated desktop container, you will need to find an alternative. Otherwise, we recommend transitioning to [Webtop](https://github.com/linuxserver/docker-webtop).

As mentioned on our [blog](https://www.linuxserver.io/blog/spring-cleaning-new-images-and-rebasing), in the repositories [issues](https://github.com/linuxserver/docker-rdesktop/issues/204), and our [info channel](https://info.linuxserver.io/issues/2026-03-18-rdesktop/) Rdesktop and it's base images will no longer be built by us or supported.
