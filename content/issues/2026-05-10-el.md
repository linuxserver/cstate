title: 'Webtop removal of Enterprise Linux tags'
date: '2026-05-10 21:00:00Z'
informational: true
affected:
  - 'Notifications'
  - 'webtop'
section: 'issue'
---

The Enterprise Linux tags for Webtop have been deprecated (el-xfce, el-i3, el-mate). These tags have fallen out of maintenance due to a lack of proper wayland integration. Attempts to base on Enterprise Linux 10 were not successful as too many packages needed for our stack are missing along with being dated now.

The baseimages for Enterprise linux have also been deprecated. 
[docker-baseimage-el](https://github.com/linuxserver/docker-baseimage-el)
[docker-baseimage-selkies el9 branch](https://github.com/linuxserver/docker-baseimage-selkies/tree/el9)
